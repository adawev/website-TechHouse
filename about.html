About Tech House

Our Story

Tech House was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to provide households across Uzbekistan with high-quality, modern appliances at competitive prices. What started as a small retail store in Tashkent has grown into a trusted brand with multiple physical locations and a comprehensive online platform.

We recognized that customers needed more than just products - they needed a reliable partner who understands their needs, offers expert guidance, and provides exceptional after-sales support. This understanding became the foundation of our business philosophy.

Today, Tech House serves thousands of satisfied customers, offering everything from kitchen appliances and cleaning devices to smart home solutions and personal care electronics.
