Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade my membership?

You can upgrade anytime from your account dashboard. The new benefits apply immediately, and unused fees are prorated.

Can I cancel my membership?

Yes, you can cancel anytime. Annual memberships are non-refundable but you retain benefits until the end of your billing period.

How do loyalty points work?

Earn points on every purchase (1 point = $1 spent, multiplied by your tier). Redeem 100 points for $10 off future orders.

Can I share my membership?

Memberships are personal and non-transferable. However, Gold members can add family members at a discounted rate.
